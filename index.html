About me_

I am age = 17 years old and student at the HTBLA Kaindorf a. d. Sulm in the third year. I chose this institution because I discovered the world of computer science at an early age. In the three years of my educational journey, I have already been able to gain a lot of experience, but I have not only learned through school, but also through the interest and motivation that drives me. So, coding has grown on me and is one of my main passions.

My interests span a wide range of technical and creative fields. On my journey through programming I got to know languages like </ C, Java, Javascript/Typescript, Python, PHP > and I also deal with web development. I am always up for new tasks and challenges. Furthermore I'm very curious, like to learn new things. Besides that, I'm also fascinated by databases, cybersecurity, AI and much more.

Outside of the digital world, my hobbies are a colorful contrast to my technology interests. Music is an important part of my life, and I play the viola and flute. I play in the wind band Grenzlandtrachtenkapelle (GTK) Mureck. Another instrument I recently learned is the piano, which further enriches my love of music. Also the world of chess fascinates me, but in the end music wins.

My personal life path is a mishmash of experiences from different countries. Originally, I come from Luxembourg, where I first attended elementary school. In 2017, my life took a significant turn when I moved to Austria to the beautiful province of Styria. I continued my education in Austria, where I completed secondary school with excellent results. This educational success gave me the opportunity to enroll at HTBLA Kaindorf.
